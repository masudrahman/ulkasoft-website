About Our Company

ULKASOFT is an international software development company covering a full spectrum of IT services, including software design, development, implementation, testing, support, and consulting.

  • We value our clients.
  • We believe in quality.
  • We value our people.
We succeed by ensuring your success.

ULKASOFT helps software companies in the United States and around the world with developing functionality and features for their products. At ULKASOFT, we are caretakers of your intellectual property, proprietary software and new development specifications. You retain full ownership of all intellectual property rights and software source code at the end of any project. We are your offshore software development partners and are fully focused on getting your project done quickly, efficiently and effectively.


Key Benefits

  • Experience of developing customized, fully integrated systems.
  • Prompt and flexible responses to any changes or additions during the course of the project.
  • Protection of Intellectual Property Rights (IPR).
  • Reduce development costs by more than 50%.

Our Strengths

  • Company vision, culture, and skills fit well for outsourcing in target markets.
  • Excellent working knowledge of English.
  • Deep understanding of various corporate identities and philosophies.
  • High transparency in management and production processes.

Project Cycle Activities

  • Well-established requirements management.
  • Good control of development process.
  • Well-established QA approach.
