FAQ

  1. Where is Bangladesh and how large is the population?

    2. Ans: Bangladesh is located in South Asia, east of India. The other neighboring countries are China, Singapore, Malaysia and Myanmar. The population is over 160 million.

  2. How well is Bangladesh connected globally?

    3. Ans: Bangladesh is well connected to global hub points, like Singapore (4 hrs), Hong Kong (3.5 hrs), Dubai (5.5 hrs), Kuala Lumpur (3.5 hrs) and Delhi (2.5 hrs) with frequent flights every day.

  3. How is the internet and communication infrastructure?

    4. Ans: The internet infrastructure is up to the mark and fit for competitive hi-tech business worldwide. The whole country is connected through a hi-speed fiber-optic backbone, supported by a number of service providers. VPN, IPLC, encrypted tunnels and other high-end secured connectivity to local and multinational companies are available to connect globally. Telephone service is also much cheaper than India, China or US.

  4. How can one be sure about confidentiality and data security?

    5. Ans: Customers have the freedom to choose their own security models here. Our options include CC camera with remote surveillance from anywhere, secured and logged entry, and VPN tunnels to all over the world. ULKASOFT has also worked through remote log-in to our customer’s overseas data centers through encrypted tunnels for years. All engineers have to sign an NDA with thorough background check and dedicated contract agreements for particular projects as per customer’s requirements. The design center is located in a diplomatic and non-commercial zone of the city which assures much more security than other areas. ULKASOFT also maintains dedicated work-chamber (cage) with 24x7 electronic surveillance and storage/server for every customer to keep their data secured. The local administration and law and order authority provides full support under Trademarks Act 2009, Main IP Laws and Copyright Act 2000 (Amended on 2005).

  5. What sort of tool exposure/access do engineers have?

    6. Ans: ULKASOFT engineers already have exposure to most of the widely used EDA tools like Virtuoso, Calibre, VCS, SoC Encounter, Assura, UltraSim, Spectre, L-Edit, and H-Spice from leading vendors like Cadence, Synopsis, Mentor Graphics and Tanner.

  6. Is Bangladesh familiar with international business culture, particularly in semiconductor fields?

    7. Ans: Bangladesh has more than 30 years of experience in the ready-made garments industry, leading it to be one of the biggest manufacturers and exporters in this sector. Thus it is already in a culture of competitive but ethical business practices. Bangladesh already has over 10 years of experience in the semiconductor industry. Within this very short period of time, Bangladesh has been associated with several big names of the arena. The customers have consistently been impressed with the work ethic, commitment, and the resulting quality of work. Many are already expanding their business here.
